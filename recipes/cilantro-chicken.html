Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed berries
  • 1 cup granola
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Honey to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a baking dish, combine the mixed berries and honey.
  3. Top with granola and yogurt.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until heated through.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
